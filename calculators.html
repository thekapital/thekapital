Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are these calculators?

Our calculators provide close estimates based on current market data and standard lending criteria. Actual figures depend on your individual circumstances, credit history and lender policies. For a precise assessment, speak with our specialists.

What interest rate should I use in the mortgage calculator?

We default to current average variable rates. You can adjust this in our Mortgage Repayment Calculator to model fixed-rate scenarios or compare different lender offers you've received.

Am I eligible for the NSW First Home Buyer exemption?

If you're purchasing your first home in NSW for $800,000 or less, you may be fully exempt from stamp duty. Properties between $800k–$1m receive a partial concession. Use our Stamp Duty Calculator to estimate your savings.

What is the APRA serviceability buffer?

APRA requires lenders to assess your ability to repay at an interest rate at least 3% above the loan's actual rate. This buffer is factored into our Borrowing Capacity Calculator to give you a realistic estimate of what lenders will approve.

Can I avoid LMI without a 20% deposit?

Yes. A guarantor home loan allows a family member to pledge equity as security, eliminating Lenders Mortgage Insurance even with as little as 5% cash deposit. Use our Guarantor Loan Calculator to see how much equity is required.

Can I use these tools on my phone?

Yes. All calculators are fully responsive and optimised for mobile, tablet and desktop devices. No app download required.
